About Culbertson

Culbertson Nebraska is a village tucked in the corner of Southwest Nebraska with a population of 573 people. It was founded in 1873 and incorporated in 1885. It served as the first county seat of Hitchcock County. Culbertson was named in honor of Alexander Culbertson, who was an Indian agent, fur trader, and partner in the Upper Missouri Outfit.

Since Culbertson is a small farming community you have less crime, traffic, and smog to worry about. Culbertson offers an elementary school, a public library, a wonderful walking trail, two restaurants, a public swimming pool, two public parks, a bank, is minutes from the Swanson Reservoir State Recreation Area and is in close proximity to McCook, NE.

Education

Hitchcock County Elementary

Hitchcock County Elementary School is located at 712 Arizona St, Culbertson, NE 69024 and is a part of the Hitchcock County Unified School System District. It currently has 161 students in grades kindergarten through sixth grade. It also has a student-teacher ratio of 13 to 1.

If you are interested in learning more about Hitchcock County Elementary, you can contact them at (308) 278-2131 or visit their website at https://www.hcfalcons.org/.

County Services

Hitchcock County Courthouse

The Hitchcock County Court House is located in Trenton, but you can access their website through hitchcockcounty.ne.gov.  The Hitchcock County website contains information about courthouse closings and events, the history of Hitchcock County, the different offices located inside the courthouse, staff at the courthouse, info about the Board of Commissioners, and links to different office services.
